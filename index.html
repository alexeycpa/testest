Chicken & Corn Salad

Description

Ingredients 2 persons 1 chicken breast Kernel corn Lettuce Salt Pepper Olive oil Paprika Cayenne Basil

Cooking

1

Prepare your chicken by slicing it into squares (bite-size) then fry in a pre-heated pan with olive oil. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir until chicken is cooked.

2

Add the corn to the pan together with the basil then add a pinch of cayenne. Add a little more of the olive oil.

3

Place the lettuce on a salad bowl then mix in the chicken & corn mixture together with the oil. Then serve.
